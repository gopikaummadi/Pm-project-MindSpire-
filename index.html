Balanced Lifestyle

Daily Rhythm for a Balanced Life

  1. Follow a Natural Routine (Circadian Living)
    • Wake up with sunlight
    • Eat meals at regular times
    • Sleep early & rise early
  2. Balance Between Doing & Being
    Action: work/goals. Being: rest/reflection. Rule: 80% output ‚Üí 20% recharge.
  3. Eat. Move. Rest. Repeat.
    Eat fresh food, move daily, take micro-rests.
  4. Mental Balance
    Limit overthinking, replace "busy" with "present". Journal to declutter.
  5. Social + Alone Time
    Create space for uplifting people and guilt-free alone time.
